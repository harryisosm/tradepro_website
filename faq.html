Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our trading services

Getting Started

How do I open a trading account?

To open a trading account, click on the "Get Started" button, fill out the registration form, and complete the verification process. Our team will guide you through the setup process.

What documents do I need to register?

You'll need to provide: government-issued ID (passport or driver's license), proof of residence (utility bill or bank statement), and additional documentation for certain account types.

What is the minimum deposit?

Our minimum deposit requirement varies by account type. Basic accounts start at $250, while premium accounts require higher initial deposits.

Trading

What markets can I trade?

We offer trading in forex pairs, commodities (COMEX), cryptocurrencies, and select global stocks. The available markets depend on your account type.

What trading platforms do you offer?

We provide access to industry-standard platforms including MetaTrader 4, MetaTrader 5, and our proprietary TradePro platform.

What are your trading hours?

Forex markets are available 24/5, while other markets follow their respective exchange hours. Check our market schedule for specific details.

Account Management

How do I deposit funds?

We accept deposits through bank transfer, credit/debit cards, and various electronic payment methods. Visit our payment page for details.

How long do withdrawals take?

Withdrawal processing times vary by method: e-wallets (24-48 hours), credit cards (3-5 business days), bank transfers (5-7 business days).

Is my money safe?

Yes, client funds are held in segregated accounts at top-tier banks and protected by industry-standard security measures.

Support

How can I contact support?

Our support team is available 24/7 through live chat, email, and phone. Premium accounts get priority support access.

Do you offer trading education?

Yes, we provide comprehensive educational resources including webinars, tutorials, market analysis, and one-on-one training sessions.

What if I have technical issues?

Our technical support team is available 24/5 to help resolve any platform or trading-related issues.

Still Have Questions?

Our support team is here to help you with any questions or concerns.
