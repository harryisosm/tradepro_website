Refund Policy

Understanding our refund terms and conditions

Refund Eligibility

We strive to provide the highest quality trading services. Our refund policy is designed to be fair and transparent while protecting both our clients and our business.

Subscription Services

Trial Period

  • 14-day trial period for new subscribers
  • Full refund available if cancelled within trial period
  • No refund for services used beyond trial period

Regular Subscriptions

  • Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable once the service period begins
  • Annual subscriptions may be eligible for partial refund within first 30 days
  • Refund amount prorated based on unused service period

Trading Accounts

Deposit Refunds

  • Unused deposits may be withdrawn at any time
  • Processing fees may apply to withdrawals
  • Verification required for all withdrawal requests

Trading Losses

  • Trading losses are not eligible for refunds
  • Market risks are assumed by the trader
  • Review our risk disclaimer for more information

Refund Process

  1. Submit refund request through our support system
  2. Include your account details and reason for refund
  3. Allow 5-7 business days for review
  4. Approved refunds processed within 10 business days

Exceptions

TradePro reserves the right to:

  • Review each refund request individually
  • Request additional information if needed
  • Deny refunds in cases of policy violation or abuse
  • Make exceptions in extraordinary circumstances

Contact Us

For refund inquiries, please contact our support team at support@tradepro.com or through our contact form.
