Payment Methods

Secure and convenient ways to fund your trading account

Accepted Payment Methods

Credit/Debit Cards

Instant deposits with Visa, MasterCard, and American Express

  • Processing time: Instant
  • Minimum: $50
  • Maximum: $10,000
  • Fee: 2.5%

Bank Transfer

Secure wire transfers directly from your bank account

  • Processing time: 1-3 business days
  • Minimum: $100
  • Maximum: $100,000
  • Fee: Free

E-Wallets

Quick deposits via PayPal, Skrill, and Neteller

  • Processing time: Instant
  • Minimum: $25
  • Maximum: $5,000
  • Fee: 1.5%

Cryptocurrency

Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other major cryptocurrencies

  • Processing time: 30 minutes
  • Minimum: $100
  • Maximum: $50,000
  • Fee: Network fees only
